About The Course

A Beginner-Friendly Path to Professional Motion Design

The Motion Design Essential Course is designed to teach you how to create professional motion and animations using just your smartphone. Whether you are a complete beginner or someone looking to improve their creative skills, this course provides a step-by-step roadmap that helps you understand the foundations of motion design without confusion.

You will learn practical techniques and industry-standard methods used to create visually appealing animations for brands and businesses.

Meet Your Coach

Guided by a Global Coach Who's Done It

My name is Femi Maven, a Motion Designer, Creative Director, and the Founder of Maven Mint Academy.

Over four years ago, I started my motion design journey with nothing but a smartphone and a determination to learn. Finding quality resources was difficult, so I spent years teaching myself through research, practice, and experimentation until I mastered the skill.

To help others avoid the same struggle, I founded Maven Mint Academy in 2023. Since then, we have trained 500+ students across 10+ countries, helping them develop industry-standard motion design skills and work with brands in industries such as FinTech, AI, Crypto, Web3, and E-commerce.

My work and contributions to creative education have earned me recognition, including the Africa Media Excellence Award under the Gen Z Awards. Today, my mission is to help more people learn motion design, build valuable skills, and create opportunities for themselves using the smartphone they already own.

I am also a public speaker and have had the privilege of inspiring many people through both virtual and in-person speaking engagements. Driven by a mission to positively impact millions, I am dedicated to making a difference and being a blessing to my generation.
